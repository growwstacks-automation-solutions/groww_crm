Questions We Hear Every Day

Honest answers. No sales fluff.

What technology powers GrowwCRM?
GrowwCRM is powered by HighLevel — one of the world's leading AI-driven CRM and marketing automation platforms, serving 1M+ businesses globally and recognised by G2 and Capterra as a top product in 2025 and 2026. GrowwStacks configures, customises, and supports it specifically for the Indian market. You get enterprise-grade software with local support.
Is GrowwCRM built for Indian businesses?
Yes, completely. We configure it with ₹ currency, Indian date formats, WhatsApp as the primary communication channel, and support in Hindi and English. Pricing is designed specifically for Indian SMBs.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within 24 hours. Our team handles everything: importing your contacts, configuring your pipeline stages, and building your first automation workflows. No technical knowledge needed.
Can I migrate from Zoho, HubSpot, or Excel?
Absolutely. We handle migrations from Zoho, Freshsales, HubSpot, Excel spreadsheets, and even WhatsApp contact lists. Our team maps your existing data and imports everything cleanly with zero data loss.
Does it work with WhatsApp Business?
Yes. GrowwCRM integrates with WhatsApp Business API to send automated follow-ups, appointment reminders, and campaigns directly from within your CRM — fully compliant with WhatsApp's policies.
What kind of support do you provide?
Dedicated support via WhatsApp, email, and scheduled video calls with the GrowwStacks team — the same people who configured your CRM. Support in Hindi & English, 9am–7pm IST. Not a chatbot.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit with enterprise-grade security. HighLevel complies with global data protection standards. We can discuss specific data residency requirements during onboarding.
